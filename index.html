Vincent Van Gogh's Famous Works

Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who is known today as one of the most famous and influential artists of the 19th century.

In just over 10 years, he painted roughly 2,100 unique artworks, 860 of which were oil paintings. The majority of his paintings were painted the last two years of his life.
