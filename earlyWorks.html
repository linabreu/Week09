Vincent Van Gogh's Early Works

Contary to what you might think, Van Gogh didn't grow up painting, in fact, he didn't start paining until he was 27 years old

1883 Van Gogh moved to the town of Nuenen in North Brabant which is located in the southern portion of the Netherlands, it was here that he painted his first work "The Potato Eaters of 1885"
